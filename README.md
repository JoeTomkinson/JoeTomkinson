## About Me

I'm a full-stack software engineer in a Head of Software Development role. I follow the T-Model approach to learning — broad experience across the Microsoft stack and beyond, with deep specialisation in integration development, AI/ML (Cognitive Services), and Windows desktop development. I have years of experience in technology strategy for enterprise software delivery in commercially focused environments, leading multiple teams through large-scale projects.

I'm the founder of [Dev in a Box Limited](https://devinabox.dev), a software studio registered in England & Wales that creates Windows desktop applications and SaaS products. You can explore what we're building on the [products page](https://devinabox.dev/products) or find our apps on the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/search/publisher?name=Dev+in+a+Box&hl=en-us&gl=US). :computer:

I also write about the journey from developer to technical leader over at [Coder to Corner Office (CTCO)](https://www.ctco.blog) — covering software engineering, AI, leadership, and career growth.

:gem: **I'm open to meaningful pro-bono work for humanitarian organisations — please reach out if that's something you are interested in discussing.**
